Frequently Asked Questions About Green Program

What is included in the Green Program at Camp Netimus, and how does it benefit my daughter?
Our Green Program at Camp Netimus for Girls is designed to immerse campers in environmental stewardship and sustainability practices. Girls participate in activities such as organic gardening, nature walks, recycling projects, and hands-on ecology workshops. The program helps campers develop a deeper appreciation for the natural world, fosters eco-friendly habits, and encourages teamwork through group projects—all set in the beautiful Pocono Mountains of Milford, PA.
How much does it cost to enroll my daughter in the Green Program, and are there any additional fees for special activities?
Tuition for Camp Netimus varies depending on the session length—our most popular 3-week session is $4,550, with the Green Program included at no extra charge. There may be small additional fees for optional field trips or special workshops, but all essential Green Program activities and materials are covered by the standard tuition. Please contact our office for updated rates and available scholarships.
When does the Green Program run during the summer, and how do I sign my daughter up?
The Green Program operates throughout the entire camping season, from late June to mid-August. Campers can participate in Green Program activities during any session they attend. To enroll, simply complete our online application and indicate your interest in the Green Program; our staff will ensure your daughter is included in all relevant activities.
Is Camp Netimus licensed and are staff trained for outdoor and environmental education?
Yes, Camp Netimus is fully licensed by the Pennsylvania Department of Health and complies with all state and local regulations for residential camps. Our Green Program is led by experienced environmental educators, many of whom hold degrees in environmental science or education. All staff undergo background checks and receive training in safety protocols specific to outdoor and environmental activities.
What happens if my child doesn’t enjoy the Green Program—do you offer any guarantees or flexible options?
We want every camper to have a rewarding experience. If your daughter tries the Green Program and prefers other activities, she can easily switch to different programs offered at Camp Netimus. While we don't offer money-back guarantees, we do provide individualized attention to ensure each camper’s interests are met, and our directors are always available to discuss alternative activity options during her session.
