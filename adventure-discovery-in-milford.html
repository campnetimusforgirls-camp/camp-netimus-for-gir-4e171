Frequently Asked Questions About Adventure & Discovery

What is the typical session length and schedule at Camp Netimus, and when does registration open for the summer?
Camp Netimus offers both 2, 4, 6, and 8-week sessions each summer. The main camp season usually runs from late June through mid-August, with exact dates posted on our website each fall. Registration for the following summer opens in September and continues until spots fill up—early registration is recommended as some sessions fill quickly.
How much does it cost to attend Camp Netimus, and what does the price include?
Tuition for Camp Netimus varies depending on session length. For example, the 4-week session is approximately $6,900, while the full 8-week session is $11,800. Tuition covers lodging, meals, daily activities, and most special events. Some optional trips and horseback riding lessons may have additional fees. A full list of pricing and what’s included is available on our website or by calling our Milford, PA office.
Is Camp Netimus licensed and accredited, and what safety measures are in place for campers?
Yes, Camp Netimus is licensed by the Pennsylvania Department of Health and accredited by the American Camp Association (ACA), which requires us to meet rigorous safety and program standards. All staff participate in extensive pre-camp training, and we have certified lifeguards, activity specialists, and a full-time nurse on-site. Our Milford location is regularly inspected to ensure compliance with all state and ACA safety requirements.
What is the process for enrolling my daughter, and are there waitlists for popular sessions?
You can enroll your daughter online via our website or by contacting our office directly. After submitting an application and deposit, you’ll receive a confirmation and additional forms for health, transportation, and activity preferences. Some sessions, particularly in July, can fill early; if a session is full, we offer a waitlist and will notify you promptly if a spot opens. We recommend registering as soon as possible to secure your preferred dates.
What happens if my daughter needs to leave camp early or is unhappy—do you offer any guarantees or refunds?
Our goal is for every camper to have a positive experience, and we have dedicated staff to support campers who are homesick or adjusting. If your daughter needs to leave early due to illness or family emergency, partial refunds may be available depending on the timing and circumstances, as outlined in our refund policy. We encourage you to discuss any concerns with us before and during camp so we can work together to support your child.
